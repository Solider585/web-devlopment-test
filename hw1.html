My opinion of Cats

I personally do not like cats much at all. I've only loved a single cat, and have liked 3 cats out of all the cats I have ever interacted with. Somewhere around 20 cats as a reference. My friend whom loves them often tries to change my mind of how I view cats with very little success. Before I met him I absolutely hated cats, except for the one cat I truly loved, may he rest in peace, but now I just do not like them but at the same time do not mind them if they are around me. I still do not trust them in the slightest though. This does not mean that cats are not cute because, they are and certainly can be adorable, espically the munchkin cat breed. The tiny little legs they have make them look so adorable that I would want to own one as a pet.
